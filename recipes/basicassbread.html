Basic-Ass Bread

Ingredients

  • Flour 1000g
  • Salt 20g
  • Yeast 2g
  • Water 575g

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, salt, yeast, and water in a large bowl.
  2. Cover and leave on the counter for 8 hours.
  3. Once the dough triples in size, split it into 2 loaves.
  4. Bake a 475 degrees in a covered dutch oven for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove lid and continue baking until reaching desired darkness of crust.
